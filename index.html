All About Mike

Biography

I was born in Cedar Rapids, Iowa in 1990. I've spent most of my life living on the South East side of the city. In school growing up I played the saxaphone, ran hurdle events in track, and participated in show choir. After high school I briefly attended Iowa State University. I also have taken some classes at Kirkwood. My jobs have mostly been in food and customer service. I'm hoping this class will allow me to get a job that I like and that will allow me to have the life I want.
